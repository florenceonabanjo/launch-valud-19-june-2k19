Build your health in East London

Who's more likely to survive a heart attack, Jane or John? Why? Why is Jane more likely to live longer if she lived in Holbon versus West Ham?
Valud is a public healthcare startup that aims to tackle the problems asscociated with these concerning questions. We strive to bridge trust
between the public and traditional medicine by providing digital bitesized insights which are culturally and socially inclusive.
We value our users.
Valud utilises big data to to engage and link users to socially prescribed services in London, UK.
We have big ambitions to expand our work globally, stay tuned 😄 🌎 ❤
